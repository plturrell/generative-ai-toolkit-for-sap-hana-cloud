Interpretable Deep Learning

Neural Additive Models combine the expressiveness of deep neural networks with the interpretability of generalized additive models, offering the best of both worlds.

Feature-wise Neural Networks

NAMs use separate neural networks for each feature, allowing us to visualize and understand the contribution of each feature to the prediction.

Interactive Visualizations

Explore the relationship between features and predictions with our interactive visualization tools, designed with Jony Ive's principles of clarity and purpose.

SAP HANA Integration

Seamlessly integrate with SAP HANA Cloud to train and deploy Neural Additive Models on your enterprise data with high performance.

Customizable Architecture

Configure the depth and width of each feature's neural network to balance complexity, performance, and interpretability for your specific use case.

Feature Importance Analysis

Understand which features are most important for your model's predictions with built-in feature importance metrics and visualizations.
